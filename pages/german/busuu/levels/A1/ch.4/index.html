Please meet... the verb!


Verbs are words that describe actions or states, e.g.
"kommen" (to come),
"sprechen" (to speak),
"lernen" (to learn),
"sein" (to be).

The bold words below are all verbs:
Ich komme aus Deutschland. Woher kommst du?

"Komme" and "kommst" describe an action: to come. Therefore, they are verbs.

(I come from Germany. Where do you come from?)
Ich spreche Deutsch. Sprichst du Deutsch?

"Spreche" and "sprichst" describe an action: to speak. Therefore, they are verbs.

(I speak German. Do you speak German?)

The ending -e


A verb next to "ich" (I) takes the ending -e.
Ich spreche Deutsch. (I speak German.)
Ich wohne in Spanien. (I live in Spain.)
Ich komme aus Deutschland. (I come from Germany.)

"Ich" is a word we use to refer to ourselves.
Ich spreche Englisch.

"ich" needs a verb ending in -e.

 I speak English.
