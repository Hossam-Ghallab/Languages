In this unit, you'll learn some phrases you can use when you meet people while you're travelling. You'll also learn some questions that people might ask about your trip.
Are you on holiday?

Are you having a good time?

I'm here on business.

Let's start by learning some questions that people might ask you.

Wie lange bleibst du?
